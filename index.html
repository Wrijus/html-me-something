What is a Geek

The word geek is a slang term originally used to describe eccentric or non-mainstream people, the word typically connotes an expert or enthusiast or a person obsessed with a hobby or intellectual pursuit. The more mainstream and urban meaning of geek is an outwardly normal person who has taken the time to learn technical skills. Geeks have as normal a social life as anyone, and usually the only way to tell if someone is a geek is if they inform you of their skills.

The Start Button

My foray into world of geek tech start with me taking small and broken electronic apart, then I became the reader of manuals so no VCR, around me, ever blinked 12:00. The first PC I owned, was one I built myself, where I did all my own maintenance, repairs and upgrades. Along the way I also pickup A/V tech ie: VCR's, TV's, audio system's, DVD/Blu ray's players, Lcd/Led tv's, and now streaming devices and home theater PC's.(Plex the one media server to rule them all)
